Every time we’re together, whether at festivals, playing games, or just curled up watching movies, you’ve brought so much joy into my life. You are my everything, and I thank the universe every day for bringing you into my life. I’m excited for all the moments we’ll create, and I am so proud of the man you are and the one you’re becoming. May God bless you and have an amazing year ahead as you deserve everything good that is coming your way. Here is to to many more birthday together, while we work towards our goals and enrich our lives. I love you 🎂🤍.
